About Aperus

Built on real-world Microsoft expertise

Aperus is a UK-based technology consultancy founded to bridge the gap between enterprise CRM capability and the modern demand for customer-first mobile experiences.

With deep hands-on experience across the Microsoft Dynamics 365 Customer Engagement stack — including Sales, Customer Service, and Marketing — we bring a practitioner's perspective to every engagement.
